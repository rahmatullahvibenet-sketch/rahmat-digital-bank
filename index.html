Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why only 98,500 NBL?
Like Bitcoin's 21 million cap, NEBULA's 98,500 supply is permanently fixed. Scarcity drives value. The fewer coins exist, the more valuable each one becomes as demand grows. This number was chosen by ARCHITECT-X as a deliberate design decision.
What is a "Neb" (the smallest unit)?
A Neb is NEBULA's equivalent of Bitcoin's "Satoshi". 1 NBL = 1,000,000,000 Neb. This allows micro-transactions worth fractions of a cent, making NBL practical even at very high valuations. Named in honor of NEBULA and ARCHITECT-X.
Can more NBL be minted in the future?
No. The smart contract has no mint function. Once the initial 98,500 NBL are created at deployment, that is the permanent and immutable maximum. Not even the owner can create more tokens.
Who is ARCHITECT-X?
ARCHITECT-X is the pseudonymous creator of NEBULA, following the same tradition as Bitcoin's Satoshi Nakamoto. Like Satoshi, ARCHITECT-X chose to remain anonymous to ensure the project is fully decentralized and community-owned.
How is NEBULA different from other ERC-20 tokens?
NEBULA combines extreme scarcity (only 98,500 supply), Bitcoin-inspired denomination system (NBL ‚Üí Neb), halving schedule, and full transparency. Most ERC-20 tokens have millions or billions in supply ‚Äî NEBULA's scarcity is its core differentiator.
Where can I buy NBL?
NBL will be available on Uniswap (DEX) from day one of launch. CEX listings on CoinGecko-tracked exchanges are planned for Phase 3. Always verify the official contract address before purchasing.
Is the smart contract audited?
Yes. A full professional security audit is conducted before mainnet deployment. The audit report will be publicly available. The contract is also built entirely with OpenZeppelin's audited libraries.

Bitcoin's value is underpinned by its algorithmic scarcity ‚Äî only 21,000,000 BTC will ever exist. NEBULA applies the same philosophy at a more intimate scale: 98,500 NBL ‚Äî forever.
